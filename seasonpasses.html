If you plan to visit the pools multiple times this summer, a season pass is for you!

Passes are sold per household. Each person will receive their own individual pass card. All pass holders will need a photo either taken or reused from previous years passes. Photos can be taken at City Hall or the Valley Junction Activity Center during regular business hours (Monday - Friday: 8 a.m. - 5 p.m.) or at the West Des Moines Aquatic Centers.
Reduced-rate season pool passes are available for income-qualifying West Des Moines residents through West Des Moines Human Services.
