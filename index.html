Discounts and Refunds
  • Our facilities do not offer rain checks or refunds.
  • If an aquatic center closes because of weather (eletrical storms or cool temperatures) between 12:30 and 5pm, it will not reopen until 5:30pm.
  • If weather conditions do not allow reopening at 5:30pm, it will remain closed for the evening.
  • For weather-related changes or closures visit www.facebook.com/WDMPR
  • Only Parks and Recreation can offer special season pass discounts such as a low income discount or the early bird discount.

Rules
  • No double riders on slides, must be at least 48in tall to ride.
  • The only floatation devices patrons may bring into the water are coast guard approved life jackets and puddle jumpers (example below).
  • No smoking products are allowed in the facilities.
  • No coolers allowed, however we do allow hydrating beverages.
  • Children under ten must be accompanied by an adult (anyone 16 or older).
  • Our facilities have rules posted in the park, and patrons are responsible for following them.

Pool Check
  • Pool checks are 10 min safety breaks that occur every hour.

At Valley View
Adult Lap Swim/Current Walk
  • This is the 5:30 to 6:30 time that occurs every Sun-Fri.
  • Activities include walking in the lazy river (w/ or against the current) or lap swimming in the deep pool.
  • Non-adults and non-participants are not allowed in the facility during this time.
